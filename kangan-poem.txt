  
        Churyan

kehtey hain keh saaz koi bhi ho
star ya gitar koi bhi ho
jeb taar dheeley per jayen 
to sur nahi nikaltey
fankar koi bhi ho
gur nahi nikaltey

magar ajab teri churyan kamal kertin hain
saat suron ki leh be-misaal kertin hain

chahey in key muqabil 
shahkar koi bhi ho
chahey in ki tadad
do ya chaar koi bhi ho
              
                      writer name
                      Muhammad Sajid Khokhar
